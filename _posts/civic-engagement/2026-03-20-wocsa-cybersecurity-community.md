The World Open CyberSecurity Association (WOCSA) is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving global cybersecurity awareness, education, and solutions through open collaboration.

## My Role at WOCSA

I am an actively involved member of WOCSA, contributing to the creation of new workshops focused on IoT. I participate in:
- Ethical Hacking workshops
- Cyberjutsu sessions
- IoT security training
- Community outreach and promotion

## Core Belief

**In cybersecurity, knowledge only increases in value once shared.**

Since joining WOCSA, I have had the privilege of leading workshops and engaging with newcomers, professionals, and cybersecurity enthusiasts. WOCSA provides me with an incredible opportunity to make a meaningful civic contribution in a non-profit organization whose values align closely with mine.
