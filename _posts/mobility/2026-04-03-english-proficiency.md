My English proficiency (B2/C1) is the result of both academic and professional experience. Language skills are essential for international mobility and professional communication.

## Academic and Professional English

English has always been a core part of my education, where I achieved a B2/C1 level. Today, I use it daily at Alsatis Reseaux, particularly for communicating with our international partners. I also draft workshops for WOCSA in English.

## Continuous Practice

Outside of work, I practice English in everyday life:
- Chatting with friends
- Watching films in their original version
- Staying updated on diverse topics and technical documentation

This continuous exposure helps me maintain and improve my fluency, ensuring I can effectively communicate in both professional and academic settings during international mobility.
