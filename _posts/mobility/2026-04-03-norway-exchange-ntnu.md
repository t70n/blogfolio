As part of ENSEEIHT's student exchange program for S9, I am applying to study at Norges teknisk-naturvitenskapelige universitet (NTNU) in Norway.

![NTNU Norway](/assets/legacy/img/ntnu_front.jpg)

## Target Courses

I plan to follow these courses:

- [Space Technology I](https://www.ntnu.edu/studies/courses/TTT4234/2025/1#tab=omEmnet)
- [Advanced Ethical Hacking](https://www.ntnu.edu/studies/courses/TTM4536/2025/1#tab=omEmnet)
- [Ethical Hacking and Penetration Testing](https://www.ntnu.edu/studies/courses/IIK3100/2025/1#tab=omEmnet)
- [Wireless Network Security](https://www.ntnu.edu/studies/courses/TTM4138/2025/1#tab=omEmnet)
- [Post-Quantum Cryptography](https://www.ntnu.edu/studies/courses/IIK8105/2025/1#tab=omEmnet)
- [Security in programming and cryptography](https://www.ntnu.edu/studies/courses/IDATT2503/2025/1#tab=omEmnet)
- [Bio-Inspired Artificial Intelligence](https://www.ntnu.edu/studies/courses/IT3708/2025/1#tab=omEmnet)

NTNU offers an excellent blend of advanced IoT, cybersecurity, and space technology courses that align with my career goals.
