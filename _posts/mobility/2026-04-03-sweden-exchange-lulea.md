## Target Courses

I would like to follow the following courses:

- [IoT-based industrial automation and digitalization](https://www.ltu.se/en/education/syllabuses/course-syllabus?id=D7042E)
- [Industrial AI for Operation and Maintenance](https://www.ltu.se/en/education/syllabuses/course-syllabus?id=D7018B)

Lulea's focus on IoT-based industrial automation directly aligns with my work at Alsatis Reseaux and my career ambitions in connected and sustainable territories.
