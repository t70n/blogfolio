Within the IoT Team at Alsatis Reseaux, I oversee the administration of equipment within the LoRaWAN core network environment and develop API scripts to optimize internal processes. This role has been transformative for my professional growth.

## My Mission

My primary mission is to contribute to improvements in:
- Automatic configuration processes for LoRa sites
- Equipment resilience, particularly gateways
- Integration of various LNS (LoRaWAN Network Servers) and monitoring tools
- Simplification of deployment, supervision, and administration of LoRaWAN networks
- Addressing critical cybersecurity challenges

## Why Alsatis

![Alsatis Logo](/assets/legacy/img/career/alsatis_reseaux_logo.png)

Alsatis has provided me with strong technical and human learning opportunities in a friendly environment. I find the human values and work ethic here align with what shaped me from the beginning of my university studies. The trust placed in me allows me to develop my skills on complex subjects while making meaningful contributions to the company.

## The Vision

The Internet of Things continues to expand through use cases that benefit society. I am motivated by combining technology with sustainable ecosystem development. Alsatis is one of the major French drivers of evolving IoT ecosystems, and I'm proud to be part of it.

## What Excites Me

What excites me most is the ability to:
- Work with cutting-edge IoT technologies
- Contribute to real-world connected infrastructure
- Solve complex technical problems
- Make a positive impact on sustainable territories
- Develop both technical and soft skills in a supportive environment

Nearly a year into this apprenticeship, I continue to be energized by the challenges and opportunities at Alsatis.
