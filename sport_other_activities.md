## Sport and Other Activities

## Climbing

After many years practicing martial arts, I discovered another sporting passion: rock climbing. I mainly do bouldering, drawn by both physical demands and mental challenge.

Every climb requires strategy and problem-solving, making it as much a test of mind as strength and agility. In the coming months, I plan to progress toward lead climbing and outdoor routes.

## Music

As a child, I started guitar at my town music school, then played in several bands through Rockschool. Later, I took up drums to explore new directions.

At ENSEEIHT, I joined a rock cover band called NightSuze, playing artists like Muse, Maneskin, Bruno Mars, and more. I am now also exploring a more classical style, including video game soundtracks like The Last of Us and Clair Obscur Expedition 33.

## Martial Arts

Raised with martial arts values since childhood, I practiced Karate Gojo-Ryu for many years under JUNDOKAN International and earned my black belt in Portugal.

This style, focused on technical excellence in katas, taught me resilience, determination, and the value of learning through repetition. Later, I moved to combat jujitsu with a strong interest in ground fighting.

## Moto

Motorcycles have always been part of my life. I got my license right after finishing my first biology degree, and my bike became my main mode of transportation.

I enjoy riding mountain trails and country roads, taking breaks in beautiful places, and discovering my country. As someone who also enjoys hiking, I am planning to start motorcycle touring, with a mid-term goal of traveling abroad on two wheels.
